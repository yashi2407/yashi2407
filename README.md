## ✶ A little about me

I’m **Yashika Ahlawat**, a Software Engineer who enjoys turning complicated ideas into products that feel simple, reliable, and useful.

I earned my **Master’s degree in Computer Science from The George Washington University (GWU)** with a **3.97/4.0 GPA** and received the **SEAS Dean’s Award**.

Before that, I completed my Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science and Engineering from **Vellore Institute of Technology, Bhopal**, graduating with a **9.22/10 GPA**.

Basically, I’ve spent several years learning how computers work and even more time figuring out why they suddenly stop working.

My experience spans **backend engineering, full-stack development, cloud infrastructure, distributed systems, machine learning, and generative AI**, shaped by more than two years of building production software at **athenahealth**.
